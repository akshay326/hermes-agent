## Key Cross-Cutting Takeaways

1. **Data alone ≠ change**: Feedback/coaching required (Browne 2021, Heizmann 2023)
2. **HRV/sleep are valid predictors**: 5+ nights needed (Grosicki 2026, Bloomfield 2024)
3. **Data CAN cause anxiety**: Andersen 2020 documents this directly
4. **Implementation intentions work**: Especially reinforced if-then plans (Silva 2018, Johansen 2023)
5. **Gamification needs intrinsic motivation**: Extrinsic-only = short-term only (Wang 2021, Cheng 2019)
6. **Self-leadership > passive following**: Active data interpretation wins (Lehrer 2021)
