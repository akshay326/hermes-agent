## 5. Failure Modes of Digital CBT Apps

## Key Cross-Cutting Takeaways

1. **Self-guided digital CBT works** (Karyotaki 2017, Tong 2024) — effect sizes comparable to guided formats
2. **Behavioral activation > cognitive restructuring** in self-guided digital (Furukawa 2018, Imai 2020, Steinberg 2024)
3. **T-F-B model validated digitally** (Chow 2023, Phukao 2023, Qu 2023) — wearable data maps onto the model
4. **Dropout is the #1 failure mode** (Linardon 2020: 24-35% attrition) — design for partial engagement
5. **Self-monitoring = #1 retention driver** (Lee 2018) — showing users their own data is critical
6. **Daily frequency supported** (Marciniak 2020, Heron 2010) — EMIs up to 5x/day effective
7. **More frequent = better** (Bruijniks 2020) — twice-weekly beats once-weekly; daily digital touchpoints well-supported
8. **Evening timing has consolidation advantages** (Gutner 2016) — end-of-day reflection may aid consolidation
9. **Chronotype matching matters** (Phillips 2024) — personalize timing when possible
10. **Wearable + CBT = stronger than either alone** (Browne 2021, Ito-Masui 2023)
